The Wines

Selected Tuscan labels, chosen for you

I
Tuscany · DOCG · Red
Chianti Classico
Born from the rolling hills between Florence and Siena, Chianti Classico is the soul of Tuscan winemaking. Bright ruby in colour, with notes of cherry, dried herbs and a gentle earthiness — warm, honest and deeply expressive of the land it comes from. One of Italy's most celebrated appellations.
II
Tuscany · DOCG · White
Vernaccia di San Gimignano
Italy's first wine to receive DOCG status, produced in the medieval towers of San Gimignano. Crisp and mineral, with delicate floral notes and a clean, refreshing finish — a rare and refined white that speaks of Tuscan sun and ancient stone.
III
Tuscany · DOCG · Red
Vino Nobile di Montepulciano
Known since the Renaissance as the "noble wine," this Sangiovese from the hilltop town of Montepulciano is among Tuscany's most prestigious reds. Rich and structured, with hints of dark fruit, violet and aged oak — a wine of character and elegance that elevates any table it graces.
The wine is selected and paired for you — one bottle per two guests, chosen to complement your menu of the evening. We take care of everything, so you can simply enjoy.
